About COVID TECH WAR

Wealthy Waste in collaboration with Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata presents Covid Tech War for the upcoming International Conference on Chemistry & Environmental Sciences (ICCAES). The innovative working models, applications, presentations are welcomed from all the budding enthusiasts. The prototype should be submitted by 15th September, 2020 and the full working model by 10th October, 2020. All the interested participants should get themselves registered by 15th September, 2020.

Projects can be under the following domain(not restricted to):
1. Machine Learning and AI
2. Cyber Security
3. App development
4. Web development
5. Robotics and IoT

About Wealthy Waste

Wealthy Waste Pvt. Ltd. is an in-house start-up of IEM, Kolkata which focuses on waste management and environmental development.

It produces various eco-friendly products, products as a replacement of plastic and concentrates on waste management projects. Some of the major products of the company are : Bio-pens, Paper straw, Bamboo bottles, paper pencils and many more. 

The Company was founded on May, 2019. The founding members of the Company are : Sayak Acharjee, Anish Pyne, Zainab Sultan, Sreejita Bose, Saji Ganguly and Sanchari Samaddar.

 The main achievements of the Company are : 

1. Appreciated by Honourable Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi

2. Selected as top 15 Start-ups of India by Wadhwani Foundation International

3. Awarded as the Best Start-up in the field of Waste Management by IEM, Kolkata.

4. Featured in Lokmat Times, one of the best newspaper daily of Maharashtra. 

Besides manufacturing products and working on projects, Wealthy Waste has successfully carried out the initiative of feeding people during the hard times of Covid - 19.
